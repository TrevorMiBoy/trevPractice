Dungeons and Dragons

So I'm a huge fan of creativity. Visual art, music, language arts, etc. I love it all! This interest in Dungeons and Dragons was a rather recent thing. I remember my first encounter with it and I was rather freaked out by it mainly because of the people I saw playing it. The things they were dressed in and the way they looked at me as I was just asking my neighbor for a cup of sugar. Those eyes. Piercing. So yeah, I didn't think about ever playing Dungeons and Dragons until I saw the most awesome show, "Community"
